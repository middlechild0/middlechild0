# 🏛️ The Evolution of The Architect

I didn’t just wake up as an AI and Systems Architect. My journey was forged through rigorous academics, cybersecurity exploration, algorithmic trading, and ultimately, building autonomous enterprise ecosystems. 

Here is the data-driven history of my developer journey, based on my repository commits over the years.

---

## 🛠️ Phase 1: The Foundation (2023 - Early 2024)
**The Era of Strict Engineering & Memory Management**

While others rushed to learn basic web development, I started with the hardest, most rigorous academic languages: **Java, C, and Prolog**.
- 📌 *Key Projects:* `JavaGoats`, `OOP`, `assignment-c-lang`, `Classwork`, `File-Transfer-Project`
- 🧠 *The Takeaway:* This is where my strict "structured environment" philosophy was born. I learned Object-Oriented Programming from the ground up, understanding exactly how memory, pointers, and data structures operate at a low level.

---

## 🕵️‍♂️ Phase 2: The Hacker & Explorer (Mid 2024)
**The Era of Networks, Web3, and APIs**

I stepped out of the academic sandbox and began exploring how the internet connects, how it breaks, and how data is secured.
- 📌 *Key Projects:* `nmap-vulners`, `BBI-node`, `MCRYPTO`, `phantom-docs`
- 🧠 *The Takeaway:* I forked NMAP vulnerability scripts (Lua) to dive into cybersecurity, explored Web3 cryptography (`MCRYPTO`), and wrote backend logic in Node.js. This phase gave me a deep understanding of network security and scalable APIs.

---

## 📈 Phase 3: The Quant & Algorithmic Trader (2025)
**The Era of Python, Data, and High-Frequency Execution**

A massive pivot into Quantitative Finance and Algorithmic Trading. I stopped building simple scripts and started building systems that handle live financial data.
- 📌 *Key Projects:* `scalping_v2`, `scalping_v3`, `Auction_Marktets_Strategy`, `stoneproject`
- 🧠 *The Takeaway:* I mastered **Python**, learned how to handle live data streams, calculate Z-Scores, and execute rapid API calls to financial markets (MT5/Deriv). This gave me the aggressive, **"frictionless execution"** mindset because in Forex, latency means losing money.

---

## 🏛️ Phase 4: The Architect (2026 - Present)
**The Era of AI, Monorepos, and Stride OS**

The culmination of everything. I combined my structural Java knowledge, my backend API skills, and my Python algorithmic logic into **TypeScript, Next.js, and AI Agent Layers**. 
- 📌 *Key Projects:* `inner-circle-ops`, `stride-intel-vault`, `personal-nexus`, `Techhive`, `webitv2`
- 🧠 *The Takeaway:* I stopped building isolated scripts and started building an entire Operating System (**Sajim Inc / Stride**). I created the `personal-nexus` and `stride-intel-vault`—officially moving from a standard developer to an Architect who builds "Documents of Proof" and orchestrates autonomous AI subagents.
